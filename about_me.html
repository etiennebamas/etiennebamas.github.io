About Etienne

Short bio

  • Post-doctoral fellow at the ETH AI Center, affiliated with the groups of Prof. Rico Zenklusen and Prof. Roger Wattenhofer.

  • PhD (2023), EPFL, advised by Ola Svensson.

  • Bachelor and master in France at Ecole polytechnique and the Parisian Master of Research in Computer Science, with a master thesis advised by Louis Esperet.

Research

I am broadly interested in Theoretical Computer Science with a focus on Combinatorial Optimization, and Artificial Intelligence. My current projects are mostly about variational auto-encoders, working together with Carl Allen.

Advising and outreach

Recently, I advised two student projects whose goal was to solve classic operation research problems using ML techniques (e.g. the stochastic vehicle scheduling problem, see the final poster of the students). I also regularly advise MSc theses on various topics (e.g. approximation algorithms, online selection problems).
